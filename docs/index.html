Features

Everything for your child's growth

Simple and intuitive — designed for parents to record and analyze their child's height development

📈

Growth Curve Visualization

Real-time vector rendering of 0–18 age percentile growth curves. Supports zoom and overlays your child's measured heights directly on the chart.

🎯

Percentile Analysis

Precisely calculate your child's height percentile among peers using the LMS algorithm. Intuitively understand their relative position and spot deviations early.

🔮

Adult Height Prediction

Based on current growth trends, scientifically predict your child's expected adult height range at age 18.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Multiple Children Profiles

Manage independent growth records for multiple children simultaneously, each with their own data and growth curve.

🌍

Multiple Data Sources

Switch between WHO, Japan MHLW, and China national standards anytime to compare percentile differences across references.

🔒

100% Local Storage

All data is stored locally on your device only. No account needed, no data uploaded to any server. Your child's privacy is fully protected.
