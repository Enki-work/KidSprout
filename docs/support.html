❓ FAQ

Q: Is my data uploaded to the cloud?
No. All data is stored locally on your device only. Uninstalling the app will erase all data.
Q: Which growth standards are supported?
WHO Global, Japan MHLW, and China national standards. Switch anytime in Settings.
Q: How many children can I add?
You can currently add up to 5 child profiles, each with its own independent records.
Q: How is the percentile calculated?
Using the internationally recognized LMS (Box-Cox power normal) method, based on official LMS parameter tables from the selected data source.
Q: How do I back up my data?
Data is stored locally on your device. We recommend backing up your device regularly via iCloud or iTunes. Cloud backup is being considered for a future version.
